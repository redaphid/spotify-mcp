## Hardstyle Search Strategy

When searching for hardstyle versions of songs from this profile, try these search patterns in order:

1. `"[song title] hardstyle"` - Most common format
2. `"[song title] hardstyle remix"` - For official remixes
3. `"[song title] hardstyle sped up"` - Zyzz/gym community edits
4. `"[artist name] hardstyle"` - To find any hardstyle remix of that artist
5. `"[song title] SICK LEGEND"` or `"[song title] BAKI"` or `"[song title] POSEIDON"` - Known hardstyle remix producers
6. `"[song title] Kratos hardstyle gym bro"` - Another prolific remixer in the library
7. `"[song title] puremind"` or `"[song title] crypvolk"` - More remix producers already liked

### Known Hardstyle Remix Producers (already in library)
- **SICK LEGEND** - Huge catalog of pop/rock hardstyle remixes
- **BAKI / ZYZZ / GYM HARDSTYLE** - Zyzz-themed hardstyle edits
